What is education?

Education is a planned activity with certain goals in mind, such as knowledge transmission or the development of skills and character. These objectives might include the growth of comprehension, reason, kindness, and honesty.

Online Education

All educational levels have embraced online learning as a result of the COVID-19 epidemic. The abrupt switch from conventional learning to 100% online learning could have an impact on how well students learn, particularly in experimental courses.
